Is a decentralized prosocial platform viable? How can it avoid the inherent tensions between providing the user with a valuable experience and enforcing some level of morality?

My discussion got into the problems with each potential implementation of a "bridge-building" platform which promotes content that is liked across ideological boundaries. At every stage, there is some hidden drawback - whims of politicians, human nature toward tribalism, and opposition to a paid model.
