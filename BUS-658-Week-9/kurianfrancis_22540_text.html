Question: How to make social media platforms fair for every participant given the platforms revenue models depends on techniques like boosting a post and algorithm reprioritises or actively prevents the posts from being seen by others? (people see more boosted posts than posts from their own network)

Context provided:

Censorship is anti-democratic and  resorting to a measure long championed by closed societies to safeguard their systems of governance.  “problematic content  is not the main problem, and thus content moderation (i.e., censorship) is not the solution. The real problem is that platforms obscure the social context of what we see, leading us to mistakenly believe that viral content reflects a broad consensus, when in reality it often reflects the views of a narrow community.”  One way to bring balance is self regulated tools like community notes to bring more context to the posts and bridge the gap.
