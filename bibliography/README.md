# Bibliography (BUS 658)

Course bibliography: papers, reports, and readings for Information Systems in Digital Times. BibTeX: [references.bib](references.bib).

## Subfolders

- [AI Foundations](AI%20Foundations/README.md) — What is AI? Neural nets, attention, transformers, foundations; landscape reports.
- [Trustworthy AI](Trustworthy%20AI/README.md) — Evaluation, safety, alignment; constitution; desiderata; benchmarks (e.g. GDPVal).
- [LLM Alignment](LLM%20Alignment/README.md) — Research on aligning large language models with human values, preferences, and intentions; RLHF, instruction tuning, safety.
- [History of LLM Training and Alignment](History%20of%20LLM%20Training%20and%20Alignment/README.md) — Historical development of language models, self-supervised learning, word embeddings, transformers, and the evolution towards modern LLMs.
- [General Social Agents](General%20Social%20Agents/README.md) — Using LLMs as social agents, human surrogates, and simulations for social science; evaluation of LLM capabilities for predicting human behaviour.
- [Agentic AI](Agentic%20AI/README.md) — LLM-based agents, interactive environments, evaluation and training of agents.
- [Model Collapse](Model%20Collapse/README.md) — Training on model-generated data, recursion, and degradation of learned models.
- [Scaling Laws](Scaling%20Laws/README.md) — Scaling laws for neural networks and language models.
- [Semantic Web](Semantic%20Web/README.md) — Semantic Web, linked data, and machine-readable meaning on the web.
- [LLM Compression](LLM%20Compression/README.md) — Compression using LLMs (LLMs as compressors) and compressing LLMs (pruning, quantization, distillation, NAS).
- [Entropy Estimation](Entropy%20Estimation/README.md) — Entropy of natural language: estimation methods, limits of corpus-based estimation, word entropy across languages, LLM uncertainty quantification.
- [Emergent Properties](Emergent%20Properties/README.md) — Emergence, “more is different,” and the hierarchical structure of science; relevance to scale and complexity (e.g. in AI and language models).
